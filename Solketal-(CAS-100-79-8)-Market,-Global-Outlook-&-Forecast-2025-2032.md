Solketal is a chemical compound formed by the reaction of glycerol and acetone. The compound has a diverse range of uses, particularly in the production of pharmaceutical and cosmetic products, where it serves as a solvent. Additionally, solketal is used in fuel additives and as a green alternative to more harmful chemicals.
