<h1>Fishing Aquaculture Nets Market Size, Demand & Supply, Regional and Competitive Analysis 2025-2031</h1>


Fishing and Aquaculture Nets are devices made from fibers woven in a grid-like structure, used for fishing and aquaculture purposes. These nets are usually meshes formed by knotting a relatively thin thread. Modern nets are predominantly made of materials like nylon, with knotless nets such as wool or silk also being used.
