Hafnium Acetylacetonate is a chemical compound with the linear formula C20H28HfO8. It serves as a valuable hafnium source, dissolving in organic solvents and acting as a key component in various catalytic applications. Acetylacetonates are extensively used in organic synthesis as catalysts and catalytic reagents, particularly in the production of carbon nanostructures. The compound is crucial in advanced technological applications such as space exploration, electronics, and lighting industries.
