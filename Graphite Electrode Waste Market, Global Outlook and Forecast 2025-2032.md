Graphite Electrode Waste Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032


Graphite Electrode Waste refers to fragments or debris generated during the graphitization and machining of graphite products. These waste materials are utilized as additives and conductive materials in various industries such as electric arc furnace steelmaking, metallurgy, and chemical industrial furnace. They play a crucial role in enhancing the conductivity and thermal properties of electrode paste, thus accelerating the sintering speed.
