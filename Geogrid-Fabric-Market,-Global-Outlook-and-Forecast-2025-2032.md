<h2>Definition</h2>

Geogrid Fabric is a geosynthetic material made of polymers used to reinforce soil behind retaining walls. It is installed in horizontal layers between wall courses, extending into the soil behind a wall to stabilize the soil, increase mass, and enhance wall stability.
