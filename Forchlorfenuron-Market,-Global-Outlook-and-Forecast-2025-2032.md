<h1>Forchlorfenuron Market Size, Demand & Supply, Regional and Competitive Analysis 2025-2032</h2>

Forchlorfenuron is a new plant growth regulator registered for use on grapes, raisins, and kiwifruit. It is a phenyl urea compound and a cytokinin that improves fruit size, fruit set, cluster weight, and cold storage in grapes and kiwifruits.
