High Strength Fiberglass Cloth market refers to the market for silicon-aluminum-magnesium system glass fiber with high strength, including S-glass fiber. This market includes products that offer high tensile strength, elastic modulus, stiffness, temperature resistance, moisture resistance, and long fatigue and shelf lives.
